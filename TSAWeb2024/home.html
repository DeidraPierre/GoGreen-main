GoGreen Your Home Initiatives Mini About

Practicing clean energy at home:

Make your home clean and comfotable to live in

  1. Turn off lights when you exit a room
  2. Be mindful of water usage
  3. Invest in solar power

Practicing clean energy on the go:

It can be difficult to remember to take care of the Earth while you're away. So, here are some actions you can take

  1. Filling up gas tank when it reaches a quarter way
  2. Carpool with your peers
  3. Pick up any trash you may see on the ground
